Calculate the ideal KPIs for a program. Start from a budget to see achievable outcomes, or set a KPI target to determine the required budget.

How to use this calculator
  1. Choose a mode: Budget → KPIs to start from a budget, or KPI Target → Budget to start from a target outcome.
  2. Enter your total marketing budget (or target KPI value) and select the Primary KPI Objective — the funnel stage your program cares most about. This determines which metric is highlighted throughout.
  3. Set the Organic Boost Multiplier — this accounts for organic/viral growth on top of paid acquisition. A value of 1.48 means for every 100 paid users, you get 48 additional organic users (148 total). Set to 1.0 if no organic lift is expected.
  4. Configure geographies: set each region's budget allocation %, CPI (cost per install — the average cost to acquire one Accelerated user in that market). Allocations should total 100%.
  5. Adjust Conversion Benchmarks to match your expected funnel rates. Reach → Accelerated is the percentage of reached users who become Accelerated (e.g., 1% means 100 people see the ad for every 1 who installs). All rates are global across geographies.
  6. Review the Projected KPI Funnel and Geographic Breakdown below. All values update when you click away from an edited field or press Enter.

Refine the marketing approach by defining channels and ad types. Allocate budget across channels and see projected contributions to the KPI funnel.

How to use this calculator
  1. Review the budget carried over from KPI Planning in the summary cards above the table.
  2. Add or edit marketing channels — set the platform (e.g., Meta, Google), ad type (e.g., Video Ad, Sponsored Content), budget %, CPI (cost per user acquired through this channel), and whether the channel targets Reach or Engaged users.
  3. Ensure channel budget allocations total 100%. A warning appears if they don't.
  4. Review the Channel Contribution to Funnel to see how each channel feeds into the overall KPI funnel, cascading through downstream stages using conversion benchmarks.

Pool marketing budgets across multiple partners. Allocate each partner's budget by geography and KPI priority, then view the combined marketing pool.

How to use this calculator
  1. Add partners with their individual marketing budgets.
  2. Select each partner's geographic focus areas (multi-select) — budget is split equally across selected geographies.
  3. Set each partner's KPI priority — the funnel stage they care most about.
  4. Review the Budget Pool by Geography table to see how budgets aggregate across partners per region.
  5. Review Combined KPI Projections for the pooled funnel outcomes and cost-per-user at each stage.

Estimate creative production costs for a program. Add assets from the rate card, configure management fees, and add localisation costs to see the total production budget.

How to use this calculator
  1. Use the Currency Converter to set the source currency and exchange rate. All rate card prices are converted to USD automatically. You can also edit USD costs directly in the table.
  2. In the Creative Assets table, set the quantity for each asset type you need. Costs are pre-populated from the agency rate card but can be edited. Use Add Custom Asset for items not on the rate card.
  3. The Agency Project Management fee is auto-calculated based on the number of videos, scripts, and banners using the agency's tiered pricing. Toggle manual override to enter a custom amount. Set Additional Revision Rounds if you expect to exceed the 3 included rounds (+15% surcharge per round).
  4. Set Internal Management Fees by entering each role's rate and their allocation as a percentage of creative cost.
  5. Add Localisation countries with the cost as a percentage of the total creative asset cost.
  6. Review the Cost Summary at the bottom for the full production budget breakdown.

Suggested Resources for CPI, CTR & Regional Benchmarks
Use these sources to inform your benchmark assumptions. Data varies by industry, platform, and time period.
  • Meta Ads Library & Business Help Center — CPM, CPI, and CTR benchmarks by region
  • Google Ads Benchmarks (WordStream / Search Engine Journal) — CPC, CTR, conversion rates by industry
  • TikTok for Business — Regional ad performance benchmarks
  • Statista — Global and regional digital advertising benchmarks
  • AppsFlyer Performance Index — CPI benchmarks by country, platform, and app category
  • Adjust Global App Trends — Install and engagement benchmarks by market
  • SocialBakers (Emplifi) — Social media engagement benchmarks by platform and region
  • LinkedIn Marketing Solutions Blog — B2B advertising performance benchmarks
Important: All benchmark data must be validated by the Programs & Marketing teams before use in planning. Published benchmarks are industry averages and may not reflect your specific program performance.
